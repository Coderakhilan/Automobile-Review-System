Royal Enfield Classic 350
🏍️ Cruiser
Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a legendary cruiser motorcycle with price starting from Rs. 1.93 lakh. The bike features a powerful 349cc air-cooled engine that delivers smooth performance and iconic thump. Available in multiple color options and variants, the Classic 350 offers a perfect blend of heritage and modern technology. It delivers a mileage of around 35 kmpl and competes with Honda H'ness CB350, Jawa 42, and Benelli Imperiale 400.

  • Strong build quality
  • Improved refinement levels
  • Better braking performance

👎Cons

  • Limited top-end performance
